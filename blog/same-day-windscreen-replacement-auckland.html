Same-Day Windscreen Replacement in Auckland

How fast we can get to you, which vehicles qualify for same-day fitting, and what the process looks like from call to completion.

Yes, Same-Day Windscreen Replacement Is Available

At Central Auckland Windscreens, we offer same-day windscreen replacement across Central Auckland for common vehicle makes and models. If you call us before midday with a standard vehicle like a Toyota, Honda, Mazda or Ford, we can usually have a technician at your location the same day.

Tip for fastest service: Call 09 636-5556 before 12pm with your vehicle make, model, year and registration. This gives us time to source the correct glass and schedule a technician.

Which Vehicles Qualify for Same-Day Replacement?

Same-day service depends on glass availability. Common vehicles with high demand usually have stock available locally:

  • Toyota: Corolla, Camry, RAV4, Hilux, HiAce
  • Mazda: 2, 3, 6, CX-5, CX-3, BT-50
  • Honda: Civic, Jazz, CR-V, HR-V
  • Ford: Ranger, Territory, Focus, Escape
  • Holden: Commodore, Colorado, Astra
  • Hyundai: i30, Tucson, Santa Fe
  • Mitsubishi: Triton, Outlander, ASX
  • Nissan: Navara, X-Trail, Qashqai

Rarer or newer models may require us to order glass from a supplier, which typically takes 24 to 48 hours. European vehicles (BMW, Mercedes, Audi, VW) often need specialist glass that is not held in local stock.

What the Same-Day Process Looks Like

Step 1: Call Before 12pm

Call 09 636-5556 with your vehicle details. We check glass availability immediately and book a technician.

Step 2: We Come to You

Our mobile technician arrives at your home, workplace or wherever your vehicle is located. No need to drive to a workshop.

Step 3: Replacement Takes 1–2 Hours

We remove the damaged glass, prepare the frame, apply manufacturer-approved urethane adhesive, and fit the new windscreen.

Step 4: Safe Drive-Away Time

We tell you the exact safe drive-away time before we leave. This is typically 1 to 3 hours depending on the adhesive and weather conditions.

Same-Day Insurance Claims

If you are claiming through insurance, same-day service is still possible. We can start the claim process over the phone, get approval from your insurer, and complete the replacement the same day. We deal with AA, State, AMI, Vero, FMG, Tower and all major NZ insurers.

What If Same-Day Is Not Possible?

If your vehicle requires specialist glass or the glass is not available locally, we book you in for the next available day. We keep you updated on delivery times and prioritise your job as soon as the glass arrives.

How Much Does Same-Day Windscreen Replacement Cost?

Same-day service does not cost extra. Our pricing is the same whether we fit today or tomorrow. Standard vehicles typically cost $250 to $500. Insurance claims are handled directly — you only pay your excess.
