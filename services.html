Windscreen Replacement

Your windscreen is a structural part of your vehicle. It supports the roof and acts as the backing for your passenger airbag. That's why we only do full replacements with correctly bonded, OEM-quality laminated glass fitted to the manufacturer's specification.

  • OEM-quality laminated glass matched to your exact make, model and year
  • Manufacturer-approved urethane bonding with full cure-time guidance
  • Rain sensor, light sensor and forward-facing camera transfer
  • New mouldings and clips for a weather-tight seal
  • Safe disposal of old glass and full clean-up
  • Workmanship guarantee on every replacement

Most windscreen replacements take around two hours. We will tell you the safe drive-away time before we leave. Read more about windscreen replacement →

ADAS Recalibration

Many newer vehicles have a forward-facing camera mounted to the windscreen for lane-keep assist, autonomous emergency braking and adaptive cruise control. When the windscreen is replaced, these systems must be recalibrated so they read the road correctly.

We will advise whether your vehicle needs recalibration and arrange it as part of the replacement job. This is not optional — driving with an uncalibrated ADAS camera is a safety risk and may void your insurance.
